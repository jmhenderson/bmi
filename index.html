OUR STORY

Welcome to the Buffalo Mountain Inn (BMI) in historic Matewan, West Virginia. Newly renovated, the BMI is a former hospital that now provides comfortable housing with four bedrooms, each with a new bathroom (three with showers, one with shower/bath combo). Bed linens, towels, access to a washer & dryer, Wi-Fi, cable TV, and a fully equipped kitchen make your stay feel like home. Nestled in the middle of the Appalachian Mts., the BMI offers a magnificent view of the trails that calls out to hikers & four wheelers across the U.S.! Parking is available in the backyard, where one can spend evenings enjoying an open fire at our rustic firepit with a cold brew and hotdogs (available at a grocery store across the bridge…a hop skip and a jump from the inn). If one prefers, there is good Southern food available at the local restaurants. Don’t hesitate to book your room(s) early, as the trail riding season is booming. As we say in Matewan to all who experience this amazing region of West Virginia…..y’all come back now!
